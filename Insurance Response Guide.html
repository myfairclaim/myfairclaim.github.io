6 Common Insurance Company Tactics

Insurance companies use predictable tactics to reduce or deny claims. Click each tactic to see expert counterstrate gies.

1. "We Need More Time to Review"

Indefinite delays and vague timelines designed to frustrate claimants into giving up. State regulations require prompt claim handling - use this to your advantage.

2. "Diminished Value Isn't Covered"

Factually incorrect denial based on misrepresentation of policy coverage or state law. Diminished value is established law - call out this bad faith tactic immediately.

3. "Your Valuation Is Too High"

Challenging professional appraisals without providing their own professional counter-appraisal. Demand they put up or shut up with documented evidence.

4. "Repairs Restored Full Value"

Claiming repairs eliminate market value loss - economically impossible and factually incorrect. CARFAX records are permanent, and buyers always pay less for accident history.

5. "Take This Settlement or Nothing"

Pressure tactics and lowball offers with artificial deadlines. This is negotiation theater - you have legal rights and they know it.

6. "You Need a Lawyer for This"

Discouraging direct claims to avoid payment while hoping you'll give up. You don't need a lawyer for diminished value claims - that's why you hired us.
